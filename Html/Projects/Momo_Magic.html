About Us

Momo Magic is an Indian chain of fast food restaurants headquartered in Latur.The chain specializes in momos, momo-filled burgers (MoBurgs) and momo-based desserts. It was established in 2023 by V.J.T.I college alumni Abhishek Kotwani, Piyush Patle and Om Mukherjee. As of February 2024, there are around 425 outlets across 69 tities in India. Momo Magic Foods Pvt. Ltd. operates 3 Brands; Momo Magic, Nepal Momo Magic(tera baap gaya tha kya) and China Momo Magic which was launched in December 2023(endsem bhok me). The company aims to be an IPO in a few years with plans to gain a global foothold, i.e., they want to compete with the likes of Domino's and McDonald's.



When did momo first appear in India?
It is believed that Momos came to India in the 1960s when a large number of Tibetans entered the country. They settled in several different parts of the country including Ladakh, Darjeeling, Dharamshala, Sikkim and Delhi all the major Momo hotspots that we know and love.
